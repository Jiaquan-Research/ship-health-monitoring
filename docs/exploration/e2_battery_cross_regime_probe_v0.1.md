# E-2

Battery Cross-Regime Probe

v0.1

---

## 1. Purpose

Observe whether battery datasets exhibit:

- collapse
- mild degradation
- no obvious instability

under cross-regime evaluation.

No theory is tested.

No evidence is upgraded.

Failure is acceptable.

---

## 2. Datasets

Battery datasets only.

NASA Battery Dataset

Oxford Battery Dataset

No other datasets.

---

## 3. Allowed Methods

Simple methods only.

Allowed:

- same-window evaluation
- cross-window evaluation
- R²
- RMSE
- Spearman

Forbidden:

- PCA
- Clustering
- HDBSCAN
- UMAP
- Koopman
- SINDy
- MoE
- Transformer
- Autoencoder

No algorithm replacement.

No architecture changes.

---

## 4. Experimental Philosophy

Same as E-1.

Observation first.

No expectations.

Positive and negative results are equally valuable.

No result is expected.

Failure is acceptable.

---

## 5. Possible Outcomes

Collapse observed

Mild degradation observed

No obvious instability observed

Inconclusive

All outcomes are acceptable.

No outcome supports Q0.

No outcome disproves Q0.

---

## 6. Output Restrictions

Allowed wording:

- Collapse observed.
- Mild degradation observed.
- No obvious instability observed.
- Inconclusive.

Forbidden wording:

- Supports Q0.
- Supports Piecewise Mapping.
- Proves Type C.
- Establishes a theory.

---

## 7. Explicit Non-Goals

This experiment should NOT:

- modify Claim Registry
- modify Concept Paper
- modify engineering architecture
- support Validation D

No theory should emerge from a single experiment.

---

## 8. Evidence Status

Expected evidence level:

Very low.

Observation only.
