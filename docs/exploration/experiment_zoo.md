# Experiment Zoo

Observation Ledger

---

## 1. Purpose

This document records exploratory experiments.

Goals:

- observation
- curiosity
- hypothesis generation

Not:

- validation
- claim support
- theory construction

Positive and negative results are equally valuable.

Failure is acceptable.

---

## 2. Experiment Rules

No experiment is expected to support Q0.

No experiment can validate or invalidate Q0 alone.

Negative results are informative.

Experiment results do not automatically upgrade evidence.

Exploratory experiments must remain independent from:

- Claim Registry
- Concept Paper
- Engineering architecture
