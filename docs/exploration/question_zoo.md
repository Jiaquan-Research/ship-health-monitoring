# Question Zoo

Observation Ledger

---

## 1. Purpose

This document records research questions.

Goals:

- question management
- curiosity
- traceability

Not:

- theory construction
- evidence generation
- claim support

Questions are valuable.

Open questions are acceptable.

Failure to answer a question is acceptable.

---

## 2. Question Rules

Questions do not imply answers.

No question is expected to support Q0.

No single question can establish a theory.

Questions should follow observations.

Questions may remain unresolved indefinitely.

Failure is acceptable.
